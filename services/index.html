Services

  • Individual Sessions

    Individual music therapy sessions can address clients' specific needs in a one-on-one setting. A board-certified music therapist uses music interventions to help an individual achieve specific goals.

  • Group Sessions

    Group music therapy sessions can address clients' needs alongside peers with similar needs. A board-certified music therapist uses music interventions to help the group achieve specific goals.

  • Adaptive Lessons

    Adaptive lessons are provided by a board-certified music therapist through structured music lessons with additional modifications in physical, cognitive, or behavioral areas to meet clients' unique needs.

Workshops also Available

Help your facility or group learn more about music therapy through a workshop or in-service. Information about music therapy along with interactive demonstrations can be provided.
